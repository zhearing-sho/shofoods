Strengthening Restaurant Systems Through Smarter Purchasing

ShoFood Supply, LLC delivers value-focused procurement, contracting, and cooperative purchasing solutions for restaurant systems nationwide. We negotiate with leading foodservice manufacturers, suppliers, and distributors to secure quality products, competitive pricing, and systemwide supply-chain efficiency.

Who We Serve

We support franchised and company-owned Shoney’s®, 7PIE®, and other restaurant system clients with purchasing programs that reduce costs, streamline operations, and ensure consistent product availability.

What We Do

Procurement & Negotiation

We secure competitive pricing and dependable supply arrangements with approved vendors.

Cooperative Purchasing

We leverage systemwide purchasing power to deliver value to restaurant brands and their franchisees.

Contract Management

We manage vendor agreements, pricing structures, and product specifications to help operators stay focused on running their restaurants.

Systemwide Support

Our programs are designed to benefit both franchised and company-owned locations through efficient, transparent, and reliable supply-chain services.

How We’re Compensated

ShoFood receives an Administrative Fee from vendors based on purchased products or services. This fee is collected by vendors—not franchisees—and supports the procurement and contracting services we provide to restaurant systems.
